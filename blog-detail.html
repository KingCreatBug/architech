Achieving luxury at a low cost

Paint an accent wall in rich dark colors. Contrast, especially if the surrounding walls are white, adds drama to the space. While you're at it, paint your interior doors a different shade.

Windows can let in a lot more light than walls.

A floor-to-ceiling glass wall isn't in your budget, but letting in lots of light is pure luxury. You allow more daylight for your health and comfort. For example, if privacy is an issue, install blinds and adjust them so that light comes in from above, but no one can see. Light paint colors reflect light, and positioning mirrors to diffuse light from windows can help distribute light in dark corners.

Do you know what a morning kitchen is?

A morning kitchen is a place where appliances and breakfast supplies are corralled. It’s a way of keeping appliances off the main kitchen countertops, which frees up space for other things in the kitchen.
